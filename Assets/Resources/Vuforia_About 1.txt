The Cloud Reco sample shows how to use the cloud recognition service to recognize targets located in a cloud database.

Key Functionality
- Manage detection and tracking of cloud based image targets
- Activate Extended Tracking
- Manage camera functions: flash and continuous autofocus

Targets
https://developer.vuforia.com/sites/default/files/sample-apps/targets/imagetargets_targets.pdf

Instructions
- Point camera at target to view
- Single tap to focus
- Double tap to access options menu
